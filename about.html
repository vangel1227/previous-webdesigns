About Me

Initially self-taught then pursued additional education at Southwestern College in Winfield, KS to refine my web development skills while serving as an Air Traffic Controller in the Air Force. I have always enjoyed analyzing problems and find solutions.

Services

I provide an array of skills ranging from visual design, database management, interaction design to prototyping. Continuous improvement and learning is what I try to achieve with each project that I encounter. Check my resume for a comprehensive skill list.

Interests

Outside of programming, I always strive to bring that same drive for improvement to my other interests. Other interests include graphic design, running, powerlifting and playing guitar. Continuous learning will always be my main goal in life.

Philosophy

My philosophy when it comes to programming is content first. They goal of all websites is to inform whether it's articles, case studies, or just hours operations. Having all the flashy designs or complex styling becomes ineffective when they don't contribute to the goal of a website.
